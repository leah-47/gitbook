# What materials are the Flowing water templates suitable for? What are the special parameters?

<div align="left"></div>

Flowing Water Material has 5 different templates. In addition to color, normal, specular, and transparency, support for adjusting the foam, flow velocity, offset parameters.

The UV module controls the flow direction and normal texture tiling of the water body, and when UV scaling is performed, the foam and normal bump effects will scale together.

> **The effect of the flowing water material is affected by the model's texture map and UVs.**&#x20;
>
> The UVs of the model itself should be continuous and square 1:1 maps are preferred.
