# Edit Account Attributes

When the owner/admin members on a team do not need to use D5 Render(they might be IT personnel or do not take part in rendering), therefore not needing to be assigned an official seat, you can edit their D5 for Teams accounts to avoid taking up the official Teams seats.

In Account Attributes, you'll find two options:

1. Editable

* takes up an official seat on the team
* supports logging into D5 Launcher and D5 Showreel platform with Teams credentials

2. Manager-only

* does not occupy an official seat and is not billed
* does not support logging into D5 Launcher and D5 Showreel platform with team credentials
* however, if the account's role is Administrator, Owner, or Group Administrator, it can still log in to the team management backend to perform some operations regarding inviting members.

> That is, **if the account attribute is 'manager-only',** logging into the **D5 Launcher** shows the identity as personal or unable to use Team identity benefits & no **Showreel** entrance shown in Myspace or unable to log into Showreel platform & you can still choose personal or team identity when logging into **MySpace**.

\[Changing account attributes] is now supported only for: Owner, Super Team Admin and Super Group Admin, three roles to modify.

* You can switch an account from "Editable" to "Manager-only" and vice versa.
* By default, when inviting other accounts to join the team, their account attributes are set to "Editable".
