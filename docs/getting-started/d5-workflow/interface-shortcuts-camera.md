## Toolbar and Shortcuts

The top toolbar provides the most commonly used features.

### Left half of the top toolbar:

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXe6UBQphkccR8wv0ChGTP-RG15CQM2arWjucNTHjf68yR0BRfsQRgXoBDrpECw5O3mnoz2XuUbV5w7xdDVjbj7qAx75FsVMoqUxbaVTyKfGeKU0KtbzvAY7CzfvWQ7bgPP-wwPx?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

“Menu” includes general settings.

“Model Import” provides a direct import option for .skp / .fbx / .3dm / .abc / .d5a files.

“Asset Library” includes high-quality models, materials, particles, HDRIs, vegetation scatter templates, and terrain templates.

“Text to 3D” utilizes AI to generate 3d models based on your prompts.

“D5 Studio” is a cloud workspace to store presets for environment, effect, scatter, brush, and path.

D5 Render also has multiple advanced features in **Menu > Preference > Widget**, including section tools, stage lights, SR rendering, and VR.

### Middle part of the top toolbar:

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdmhTeTDJ2tAoHLJtqzetXJpd7QhGrgTWiXiE7sdvZ6HvGCjuGMI-tIUrOhuHi1UGj6c0NYJZO4wHogVA94D2y_45rFp3AcwT6lX7ZH3whvPYmTVGD6r7iB018C1Tz0zYGQJRcr?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

“Light source” provides an array of artificial light sources.

“Path tool” works wonders when you have to arrange characters, plants, or vehicles on a straight or curved line.

“Plant tools” is a tool kit to efficiently add plants.

“Add particles” enables you to add animated particles such as birds, fire, and streams.

“Advanced camera” supports fixing the camera at a specific angle in the PiP window when you move around in the viewport. [Explore more camera settings >](./interface-shortcuts-camera.md)

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdFdNShH5JkTzIuH8JnuuRuzGTIHXUGu2xV1_aEye5temQTXk9drWZErFCeGuVLsyrUD2w1rb9CIHzDh9y2u2tCDx_3j2sjDfALMDjBu9CkKtphkuzJIbe7ePGfIU5yQ5Ykg856?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

“Section plane or cube” enables you to section a model and review its inside structure.

&#x20;“Scatter” is a procedural plant scattering tool. You can effortlessly create delicate landscape design using this feature.&#x20;

“Terrain” system allows you to manually sculpt terrains or rapidly generate terrains from built-in templates.

### Right half of the top toolbar:

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcpftOYnj4pv5INmoLmsfG9fRAJzCpO074XXuKkP0FNLLl2hfGyHJWJdAX-49qj3Qm1SJ417DCedhywuV8Qo8ijy-fgNzMXt5S0HpytNJdUErf1Vezzpbyq5UsQkjT0KRRTNVfB?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The right half of the top toolbar provides features relevant with rendering outputs.&#x20;

You can export virtual tours, panoramas for VR, image, and animation. D5 supports adding output tasks to the render queue for batch rendering.

[How to export virtual tours >](https://www.d5render.com/posts/virtual-tour-d5-render)

[How to export images and animations >](./image-animation-output.md)

You can also post-process rendered images using the built-in [Post-Ai tool](./ai-features.md).

## Camera

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdGFqwkur54QpvlXlK7u7glK0NU9R5UDzdjtt1kxr-e5J1l4iYPCqQuCOw52SuFcGedOGYezNG6qRYpAvYNshhYXcuQJyjM9Y-unHu96LDR-PPFSrGLc8Oit9DoIfsplXsajU0?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can click the camera icon nestled within each scene to activate the relevant camera.&#x20;

It enables you to set exposure, perspective mode, aspect ratio, focal length, camera clipping, and many other elements.

You can fix a PiP window in the bottom right corner to conveniently check the adjustments.
