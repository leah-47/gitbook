# AI Features

> Project inspiration in this doc: Koala Heaven
>
> Architect: Mart Studio (<https://www.martstudio.de/> )
>
> Visualization: LINK

D5 Render's AI tools elevate each step of the visualization journey.

* **AI Spatial Concept Generation**

[D5 Hi](https://d5next.ai/) is D5 Render’s AI spatial concept generation tool. Input prompts to generate conceptual images for your model.



* **AI Material Workflow**





Make Seamless: One click to remove seams between texture maps for material perfection.



Ultra HD Texture: Upgrade texture resolution up to 4K, eliminating noise and imperfections from fuzzy and grainy maps.



AI Texture Map Generation: Import your diffuse map into D5's base color map slot, and D5's AI will generate corresponding normal, roughness, and height/AO maps.



* **AI Atmosphere Match**

Simply upload a reference image and the AI Atmosphere Match feature will auto-integrate the weather, sky, and post-production effects of the reference into your project.





* **AI Post-production**

AI Enhancer: Enhance various elements within your renders, such as lighting, materials, characters, fog, and vegetation.<br>



AI Style Transfer: Transform your renders to different stylized/realistic styles with one click. No need to adjust models or materials.
