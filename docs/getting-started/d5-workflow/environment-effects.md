### AI Atmosphere Match

D5 has a built-in AI feature to automatically generate sky, weather, and post-production effects from reference images.

Simply choose a reference image to integrate its environmental settings into your current view.&#x20;



### HDRI, Geo and Sky

Both AI-generated or preset environment settings can be adjusted to improve outcomes.&#x20;

D5 Render supports both HDR and Geo and Sky for natural lighting.

#### HDRI

You can choose an HDRI from our library or import your own HDRI background.<br>



**Skylight intensity** defines the brightness of the light emitted by the HDRI.



**Background intensity** determines the brightness of the HDRI background itself. It has a remarkable influence on the water reflection in this project.



**Rotate the HDRI** to an appropriate angle to create beautiful lighting and reflections.



**Temperature** influences the color tone of the sky light/background/both.



The '**Custom Sun**' option enhances sunlight when your HDRI doesn't have a pronounced sun. 'Sunlight Intensity' determines the brightness of the sunlight, and 'Sun Disk Radius' controls the softness of shadow edges.



You can also change the sun’s position by rotating the HDRI or adjusting the altitude/azimuth.



The weather panel enables you to easily adjust fog, wind, rain, snow, and cloud(only for Geo and Sky).



#### Geo and Sky

You can also select Geo and Sky to simulate local lighting conditions. Simply input the date and location as needed, and adjust the time to observe the lighting conditions throughout the day.



Turn on the "Cloud" feature and there'll be appealing reflections on the water surface.



### Effects

D5 provides professional post-production effects including exposure, contrast, slope, shadow, among others.
