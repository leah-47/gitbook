D5 Render offers multiple intuitive tools that help you effortlessly create lifelike environments and preview your architectural designs under various weather conditions.

### Presets from D5 Studio

> *\*D5 Studio is a cloud workspace where you can save the environment, effect, brush, path, and scatter settings after adjusting them as you like.*

We prepare a dozen ready-to-go environmental presets in the D5 Curated section.

Double-click to download and apply your desired preset. The scene instantly comes to life.

### AI Atmosphere Match

D5 has a built-in AI feature to automatically generate sky, weather, and post-production effects from reference images.

Simply choose a reference image to integrate its environmental settings into your current view.&#x20;

### HDRI, Geo and Sky

Both AI-generated or preset environment settings can be adjusted to improve outcomes.&#x20;

D5 Render supports both HDR and Geo and Sky for natural lighting.

<mark style="background-color:green;">Watch this tutorial for realistic natural lighting:</mark>&#x20;

[<mark style="background-color:green;">How to render realistic environmental lighting for exterior scenes | Render Tutorial</mark>](https://youtu.be/sUdlLDxL0AM?si=YaXRuax5uyRiF5qB)

#### HDRI

You can choose an HDRI from our library or import your own HDRI background.<br>

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfepsOcNfNsgptog2RghSiUOJ7da2oMbvAk5IOwAGSdLA1XPslXk0NBDSDO9RJhSBZL8SvJx3gzOPnpdmgFoicuL8rQVK2V1h5rCnt5AZFA8tLYGlAGMXiCeaMnxeqdrVoleqw-?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt="" width="188"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Skylight intensity** defines the brightness of the light emitted by the HDRI.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcZelmcDaT6iaML89khBAbqGi62ZfkKtwRCbF5M-q8MRsuAqaYzu79RyqQ5rt0OJclQuFVlBe4sJPZrz7SacI58B5CJ2e8_2N8XQy487UaVWipaDpJpsSqj0Abc4wFEpiKKSG1R?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Background intensity** determines the brightness of the HDRI background itself. It has a remarkable influence on the water reflection in this project.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeyZ_O_DZO69p8ryc_7GdSl_bBucSI7lfgEBQsf40S6Vl3ai7BFHSPf_b-5rY16qKhxbvgvL71Z2Fo2K9YZWrK_MxSiHMpft0SvOlEIHxFEz_8I29c8SvygqgEYNUlaa5-iG2Q5?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Rotate the HDRI** to an appropriate angle to create beautiful lighting and reflections.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXedSYjVMPw8ubH89EW7y6qi4y-4rz_PLa6O8K_lQT19t7tWVked6oIUGVL_MQq3-vgMSNsmJDJ2es_staLwBRgnWgRCa65OUDUHBbkCuvcB50Zy8tp1Q-YdxlXeCdOJbhVwEIM?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Temperature** influences the color tone of the sky light/background/both.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeJpRlnR3jUWFgfDv3BCZGb0IQh2NWubsEnpAj9PMQeTCajzaz33aF5TvGqZKeVZ8NiuN1sAmSgYCdYAG5g8muctj-VK85OY2ZwsIKaBW9gENRn7seqGyQneRW2hvcO1u-jS9XE?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The '**Custom Sun**' option enhances sunlight when your HDRI doesn't have a pronounced sun. 'Sunlight Intensity' determines the brightness of the sunlight, and 'Sun Disk Radius' controls the softness of shadow edges.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdRXhKMYLnvLid6ss1q-DEsInAOSDuyS1kVvNQJbZlF9DzA5nEoQwOmJrljmGNINZOb72AdUUVT6VP8xk17AWWDD2sJjWzhnkUqn5olqKvxX0GnwyiVs67C_CwKE17QHlOyTOz6?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can also change the sun’s position by rotating the HDRI or adjusting the altitude/azimuth.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfRN1XTavQD1fSJ10_60R_v6yxBmmUSVI-7u01u7gmckAvDS6IvOyZzGXUN4wc7Q7xFAuB70L_ZsZjl6wvQZjPFZeEp4hm1YXy_NXFACuFpVx7HcSTSFt2CtSnoFN6l4lYU5qQ1?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The weather panel enables you to easily adjust fog, wind, rain, snow, and cloud(only for Geo and Sky).

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd21fAl_DDuV2qHUGGJ2JgH1Kq9c84XGA_sTYcviVicVQggllJt9hU8o8gz8tof8KK0sP4LLVIv4YIO3C6WxMmlwlgOVMAJmGwWfe87CtufyP73f13FNHalHQ3Wd26lbuqBMdg4?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Geo and Sky

You can also select Geo and Sky to simulate local lighting conditions. Simply input the date and location as needed, and adjust the time to observe the lighting conditions throughout the day.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdE6Epk1Tx7eCJGmmeHfn-dr2U79XGu66DETuIFWvCe1mhtzUYfdXEnxYIe5Dq_ny-89I8C_0ySKVB1zx0Nds6F66f287rtCT_c4_9sSAWvEbTy9SKeEtx4y-g6WZYUWfhsmZw?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Turn on the "Cloud" feature and there'll be appealing reflections on the water surface.
