Once completing the primary modeling process, you can assess the structure's look with its materials accurately shown, which assists in decision making.

With the livesync plugin enabled, material adjustments in DCC software are updated to D5  in real time. To make things easier, try using different colors to identify each material in DCC software. This way, you can effortlessly assign them distinct textures in D5 Render.&#x20;

> In D5 Render, press the shortcut key “I” to activate the material editing panel.

Click the “Assets” button on the top toolbar to open D5 Asset Library.

### Method 3: Customizable Material Templates

<mark style="background-color:green;">Watch this tutorial to learn more about D5’s material templates:</mark> [<mark style="background-color:green;">Discover the fun of real-time PBR material rendering at D5 Material Museum!</mark>](https://youtu.be/oje02o0l4HI?si=8fgti89J8WoS2Juu)

If you are a visualization expert who prefers to craft your materials from the ground up, our material template system is perfect for you.&#x20;

It comes with 11 commonly used templates plus a customizable option, giving you all the flexibility you need.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfXXEdDMweU-DoNZJbhw1Zf_m43gb2wAvSZiuUMPOpa7SbIH0TuyQkvhzhO52uT6kIONv8SMhJ0aW8CAdLVS4gD_UmixjKu9fdmcWTV7sXUOa2qTyY-C7WzMNxMD_RpKF3Kf1Lz?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Each template has several maps for you to tweak and achieve realistic results. We’ll demonstrate how to create a walkway material.

#### - Base color or base color map

They define the main color and pattern of your material.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfAsPXlsDwoDOYoEcxiWSwdJsr9VX5ceun3SpDgaIOzBSPrFwYg28LqmfOp6rsaHfKAqqssCncdEh_RHNBhhQMt7nRkrQ7M9ON9TEAasyN3jC92hk3HF-zte57oCxgf72yNrcXl?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### - Normal map

If the material looks flat-out smooth, try adding a normal map which can enhance the gaps to create an uneven surface.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdyLxTbpSGkYUKwsMjYGtc4oYLzim3j1593a3ClgrIY0Enr0Pv3xpuD0Zh18M5iBkwCCKfQqvc257Cz6thkCCJyzBhzZDKxp6XGabgxmubzYeAUm3KUftQtjQEZPxYxPgAXZF8?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### - Specular

It makes the material glossy to better catch and reflect light. If you need to create materials like mirrors, increasing this parameter value is a good choice.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdvyWllBXgC5lvL2UObWmQ4zflhH99-KMA7KDm13Wyw14g5YLR8MhdG5k61u1GSEQzuADpM4y-3NF83UfXB1bp0KOZr9NKJc-YuK8kHXmjKwDMyLLSuLtDsWxtC6waQ6mkSvRJb?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### - Roughness

It decides how matte the material looks.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdwhU-1KKCdylj5EDD9kNgWKsvj9efxAD957CYD5XHpyALx-68av6RBsKZNV3kmjz8KF60ypjvz6OyWQJRjOd9jG4mdOoQNbSVHOrlDFc_UJAi8dtIWJA3mVHpLHt5wfn8_iag?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### - AO

An AO map can enhance depth and shadows.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfSCcSsJ_S73IiaWYXrwSEvx3sRGdXjb_M1d3Dp6DcQ9rGFx6bjBO0I77ALEmNiWjQBc_YeDT0VtqvI_Ck84ghMQpL9d2JmurCt71EA6MDSkO0FNIux1Kuc7YEdOZh0ec6t2LA?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### - Material UV

Simply put, the larger the UV stretch value, the more repetitions of the texture pattern per unit area, making the pattern appear denser.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc9qlDqFVuMtcFSdi5jIwTWe8z_hiRBCQWvZ8zuNXsYghHoXkww91acRT6Gn1-0o2e_x1NjkqcynVGAFL2g_B_sgWdOahZRyguyUU1PuaoRBKV_oiaVoBTl2yZvM8Cst9Jlzx0?key=Fj3gEvh4f7dQWs61CsH4SzuA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
